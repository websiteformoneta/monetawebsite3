Cloud Resellers Are Expected to Do More

Cloud customers expect more than infrastructure. They expect guidance on how to manage cloud costs and improve cost efficiency.

For resellers, this creates both a challenge and an opportunity. Delivering Cloud FinOps requires consistent access to accurate billing data, clear cost visibility, and the ability to identify and implement savings opportunities across customer environments.

They do not handle customer invoicing, reseller pricing, or margin management, leaving critical parts of reseller operations unsupported.
