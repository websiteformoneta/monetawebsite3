Use these exact hex codes for all frontend styling to maintain the fintech aesthetic.

* **Dark Blue Base:** `#111D2D` (RGB: 17, 29, 45)
* **Light Blue Accent:** `#3B94D0` (RGB: 59, 148, 208)
* **Orange Accent:** `#F36A22` (RGB: 243, 106, 34)

### Secondary Colors
* **Red:** `#F0141B`
* **Yellow:** `#FFBE29`
* **Green:** `#78950E`
